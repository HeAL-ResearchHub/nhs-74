Hospital Beds by Specialty — AHA

Inpatient bed capacity by specialty across Area Health Authorities.

Overview

This dashboard shows bed capacity by specialty for each AHA. Use it to assess how inpatient infrastructure is distributed, and to compare capacity profiles against consultant headcounts and Top-5 rankings.

Reading the Chart

Compare whether the specialties with the largest bed capacity in an AHA align with local consultant staffing. Persistent gaps can reflect cross-AHA referral patterns, historical centralisation, or planned specialisation.

Key Observations

  • Large bed bases without matching consultant headcount (or the reverse).
  • Neighbouring AHAs sharing similar capacity profiles—possible shared catchments.
  • Specialties that rarely appear with significant bed capacity—likely centralised elsewhere.
